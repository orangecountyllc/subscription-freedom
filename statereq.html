Maryland Minimum Auto Insurance Requirements

All drivers in Maryland must carry the following minimum auto insurance coverage:

Insurance Requirements

  • Bodily Injury Liability: $30,000 per person and $60,000 per accident
  • Property Damage Liability: $15,000 per accident
  • Personal Injury Protection (PIP): At least $2,500

These are the state-mandated minimums to legally operate a vehicle in Maryland.
